It would be great if the psjournals app included information on turnaround times and acceptance rates at political science journals. This is one of the most common feedback that I receive from its users, and I could not agree more.

These statistics are unfortunately unavailable for many journals.

The journals published by Taylor & Francis are now a welcome exception. The publisher has recently started to provide statistics on journal turnaround times and acceptance rates on its website — in separate sections for individual journals.

This blogpost brings these statistics together, collecting the data with the code available on GitHub.

All turnaround times indicate the median, measured in days. Hover over the column headers for their definitions. Furhter details are available on the publisher’s website.

See also the graphs that summarise the statistics at the end of the post.
