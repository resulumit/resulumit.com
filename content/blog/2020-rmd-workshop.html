My writing workflow has been based on R Markdown for some time. In March 2020, I organised a workshop at Campus Luzern, to share my experience with researchers from the three universities in the Swiss Canton of Lucerne. It went very well.

Since then I have revised the workshop material, which you can find at https://github.com/resulumit/rmd_workshop. The slides are better viewed at https://resulumit.com/teaching/rmd_workshop.html.

I would be happy if other instructors used my material for teaching. I would also be happy to delvier this workshop again at different institutions.

My next workshop is again at Campus Luzern, this time on creating academic websites with Blogdown. I look forward to it.
